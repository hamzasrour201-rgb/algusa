Who We Are

Federally regulated.
Industry trusted.

Since 2006, American Logistics Group LLC has been a federally regulated and bonded freight broker, delivering comprehensive logistics solutions across the United States and Canada. With top-tier ratings from IANA, TIA, and Blue Book, we've earned the trust of 5,000+ clients through unwavering reliability and deep industry expertise.

  • Federal regulation & full bonding
  • IANA & TIA top-tier member ratings
  • 100,000+ successful load deliveries
  • Dedicated account management

We leverage the best logistics technology to deliver real-time visibility, smarter routing, and seamless communication at every step.

Aljex TMS

Our Transportation Management System provides end-to-end load management, real-time tracking, automated billing, and carrier compliance monitoring — all from one powerful platform.

  • Real-time load tracking & visibility
  • Automated carrier payments
  • EDI integration
  • Advanced reporting & analytics

Truckstop Diamond Broker

As a Diamond-rated broker on Truckstop, we access the nation's largest freight marketplace — connecting us with thousands of verified carriers for optimal load matching at competitive rates.

  • Diamond broker status
  • Access to 300,000+ carriers
  • Rate intelligence tools
  • Carrier verification system

DAT Power Freight Analytics

DAT Power provides unparalleled market intelligence — freight rate benchmarking, capacity analysis, and lane-by-lane data to optimize the economics of every shipment we handle.

  • Market rate benchmarking
  • Load-to-truck ratio data
  • Lane-specific analysis
  • Historical trend intelligence

SaferWatch

SaferWatch enables us to instantly verify carrier safety records, insurance certificates, operating authority, and compliance status — ensuring your freight is always in trusted hands.

  • FMCSA data monitoring
  • Insurance verification
  • Safety score tracking
  • Automated compliance alerts

8x8 Work Cloud Communications

8x8 Work keeps our team connected with clients and carriers through HD voice, video conferencing, team messaging, and an integrated contact center — 24/7 responsive service.

  • HD cloud calling
  • Video conferencing
  • Team messaging platform
  • Contact center integration

Blue Book Services

Blue Book membership certifies our financial integrity and industry standing — providing verified ratings that signal trust to carriers, shippers, and partners across the supply chain.

  • Industry credentialing
  • Payment performance ratings
  • Business reputation reports
  • Dispute resolution support
